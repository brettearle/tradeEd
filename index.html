Platform

  • Set Up Your Training

    A easy to build curriculum for your training. Connect a video. Write some key point questions and answers. Add notes for your team. All in a subject then class by class breakdown

  • Use Your Training

    Your team jumps on. Picks a subject to learn about. Watches the video/reads the notes. Answers the quiz. Review using flash cards if they have already completed the lesson.

  • Update

    We stive to be the best partner for in house training. Continue to build on the first lesson and subject even once live. Allows you to update content and training in real time, Issues solved in minutes rather than requiring a software team to implement changes
